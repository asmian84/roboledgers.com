AI Vision

üîç AI-Powered Receipt OCR & Auto-Matching

What it is: Take a photo of any receipt with your phone, and AI instantly extracts all data (vendor, date, amount, items) and automatically matches it to the corresponding bank transaction.

How it works: Computer vision + OCR + machine learning identifies receipt layout, extracts text, parses structured data, and uses fuzzy matching to link to transactions.

Value: Eliminates manual data entry completely. Perfect for expense reports and tax documentation.

Future Vision:

AI learns your receipt patterns and can predict missing transactions. Integrates with email to auto-import digital receipts from Amazon, Uber, etc. Builds a complete digital receipt archive with full-text search.

AI Analytics

üß† Smart Business Insights Engine

What it is: AI analyzes your spending patterns and provides actionable insights like "You're spending 30% more on office supplies this month" or "Vendor A is 15% cheaper than Vendor B for the same items."

How it works: Time-series analysis, anomaly detection, comparative analytics, and natural language generation to create human-readable insights.

Value: Proactive financial management. Discover savings opportunities and cost overruns before they become problems.

Future Vision:

AI becomes your virtual CFO, providing daily briefings, alerting to unusual patterns, recommending budget adjustments, and even negotiating better vendor terms based on market data.

Automation

üìã Automated Tax Preparation & Filing

What it is: One-click generation of complete tax forms (T2125, Schedule C, etc.) with all transactions pre-categorized and ready for submission.

How it works: Transaction categorization mapped to tax codes, automatic calculation of deductions, integration with tax software APIs for direct filing.

Value: Saves thousands in accountant fees. Eliminates end-of-year scramble.

Future Vision:

AI tax advisor that optimizes deductions throughout the year, suggests timing strategies for income/expenses, identifies eligible credits, and files automatically. Handles multi-jurisdiction taxation for international businesses.

Predictive

üìä Predictive Cash Flow Forecasting

What it is: ML model predicts your cash position 30, 60, 90 days into the future based on historical patterns, recurring transactions, and seasonal trends.

How it works: Time-series forecasting (ARIMA, Prophet), machine learning on historical data, integration with scheduled payments and invoices.

Value: Prevent cash shortfalls. Plan major purchases. Optimize payment timing.

Future Vision:

Monte Carlo simulations showing probability ranges, scenario planning ("what if revenue drops 20%?"), automated alerts when cash is projected to go negative, integration with credit line APIs for automatic drawdowns.

Automation

üîÑ Subscription & Recurring Charge Tracker

What it is: Automatically identifies all recurring charges (Netflix, Spotify, SaaS tools), tracks price changes, alerts to renewals, and calculates total monthly/annual spend.

How it works: Pattern detection for regular charges, natural language processing to identify subscription services, price change monitoring.

Value: Never forget to cancel unused subscriptions. Catch unwanted price increases. Optimize subscription stack.

Future Vision:

AI suggests cheaper alternatives, bundles savings opportunities, automatically cancels unused services (with approval), negotiates better rates with vendors, creates subscription budgets.
